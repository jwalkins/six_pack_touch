About Six Pack

Six Pack Touch Rugby Club was founded in 2007, the brainchild of Mikey Short and Iain Gray, for the inaugural Edinburgh Touch Superleague season. The aim was for the younger touch players in Edinburgh to play together to help develop their skills and aim towards international honours.

Over the following seasons, Six Pack developed into a force across Scotland, taking part in the Touch Superleague and entering various one-day tournaments. Early success was achieved, with notable wins including the Edinburgh Open in 2009 and the Touch Superleague Advanced league in the same year. Over the following years success continued with further league wins in 2010, including a season-long sweep in one-day tournaments leading to a Scottish Touch Series victory in 2010.

Further STS victories continued in 2013 and 2014, including top spot at the latest Edinburgh Open this year.

Playing for Six Pack has given a large number of players the development and training required to progress to international honours. Playing, either at open or age group level, for Scotland has led to great success for many and we hope to continue to be able to provide players to the Scotland set-up in the future.

As well as leagues and one-day tournaments, Six Pack are keen Active Touch players over the colder months, with success over the 5 years we've been playing. Six Pack are also keen to progress, so training is also a key part of what we do. Although we know how to defend, we're much more happy throwing the ball around and attacking in our free-flowing kind of way!

It's always important to relax though, so don't be surprised if there's a drink or four after a tournament or after a long season!

If this sounds like the kind of club you would like to be part of, we want to hear from you! Take a look over our website, and if you've got any questions please feel free to be in touch!
